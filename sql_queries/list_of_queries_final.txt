This file lists queries that are present in both original and rewritten SQL files, with a short description for each.

U-Series (Union)
- U1: Union of top parts by available quantity with top suppliers by available quantity.
- U2: Union of top suppliers from Germany with urgent-priority customers.
- U3: Union of customers from USA, high-quantity parts, and nations starting with 'B'.
- U4: Union of USA customers, Canadian suppliers, high-quantity parts, and high supply-cost parts.
- U5: Union of recent customer orders with older high-quantity, high-price line items.
- U6: Union of top clerks by total price with nations containing 'UNITED' by total account balance.
- U7: Union of recent line items with parts having low supply cost.
- U8: Union of customers with few recent orders and line items with highest average quantity.
- U9: Union of BUILDING-segment customers with high balances and very high-balance suppliers.

O-Series (Outer Join)
- O1: RIGHT OUTER JOIN of customers and nations; counts per customer/nation.
- O2: LEFT OUTER JOIN counting low-quantity items by ship mode and priority.
- O3: FULL OUTER JOIN summing customer account balances for finished orders.
- O4: Multiple OUTER JOINs retrieving part, supplier, and nation details.
- O5: RIGHT OUTER JOIN selecting parts with specific size and availability.
- O6: Multiple OUTER JOINs variant with different ordering.

A-Series (Aggregate)
- A1: Counts line items by ship mode under date and price constraints.
- A2: Counts orders by priority within a specified date range.
- A3: Selects line items with complex date and availability rules.
- A4: Counts 'waiting' items per supplier for finished orders.

Nested/Misc
- N1: Counts suppliers per part (brand, type, size).
- Alaap: Custom revenue calculation by market segment and shipping priority.
- Nested_Test: Finds suppliers from France providing 'ivory' parts with sufficient stock.
- paper_sample: Union combining high-value customers with suppliers based on average price.

F-Series (Fragmented)
- F1: Union of JUMBO container line items with specific-range customers.
- F2: LEFT OUTER JOIN listing nation names and customer account balances with filters.

TPC-H Modified (MQ)
- MQ1: Pricing summary report for shipped line items (aggregations by flags/status).
- MQ2: Minimum-cost supplier in a region for a specific part.
- MQ3: Top unshipped orders by revenue for a market segment.
- MQ4: Counts orders by priority for a given quarter.
- MQ5: Revenue by nation for suppliers in a region during a period.
- MQ6: Estimated revenue impact if discounts were removed for low-quantity items.
- MQ10: Customers with returned items and their revenue.
- MQ11: Most valuable stock by comment within a specified nation.
- MQ17: Average extended price for small-quantity orders for a specific brand/container.
- MQ18: Large volume customer orders with specific phone prefix.
- MQ21: Suppliers who kept orders waiting (late shipments) for finished orders.

Standard TPC-H
- TPCH_Q9: Profit by nation and year for parts with names containing 'co'.
- TPCH_Q13: Customer distribution by number of orders.

Extended TPC-H (ETPCH)
- ETPCH_Q1: Pricing summary across flags/status (ETPCH variant of Q1).
- ETPCH_Q3: Top-revenue unshipped orders for 'FURNITURE' segment.
- ETPCH_Q4: Late shipments counted by priority for a quarter.
- ETPCH_Q5: Total revenue from suppliers in 'ASIA' region for a year.
- ETPCH_Q6: Total revenue from discounts for a date range and quantity constraints.
- ETPCH_Q6_1: Web-only variation of ETPCH_Q6.
- ETPCH_Q6_2: Variation summing total revenue before union.
- ETPCH_Q7: Trade revenue between FRANCE and GERMANY across channels.
- ETPCH_Q9: Profit by nation and year combining web and store channels.
- ETPCH_Q10: Top customers by revenue who returned items.
- ETPCH_Q12: Late shipments by ship modes across channels.
- ETPCH_Q14: Promotional parts market share percentage.
- ETPCH_Q15: Supplier with maximum total revenue for a month.
- ETPCH_Q21: Suppliers in ARGENTINA late on complex web orders.
- ETPCH_Q23: Cities and brands involved in cross-channel return pattern.
- ETPCH_Q24: Complex query for customer cities based on returns, orders, and stock.
